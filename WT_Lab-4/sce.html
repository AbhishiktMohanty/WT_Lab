School Of Computer Engineering


With the explosion of information and ever-increasing demand for computational sophistication in a networked world, the School of Computer Engineering aims to impart cutting-edge skills through research and development, industrial consultancy, in frontier areas of Computer and Information Technology and teaching.





ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

BACHELOR'S DEGREE

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Information Technology

    • Duration: 4 Years

---------------------------------------------------------

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed 10+2 examination in 2015, 2016 or 2017 with at least 60% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics taken together. Should have studied in regular full time formal education in their school / college. Should have been born on or after 01.07.1997.

MASTERS'S DEGREE

  • Computer Science & Engineering (Spl- Computer Engineering)
  • Computer Science & Engineering (Spl- Information Security)
  • Computer Science Engineering (Spl- Data Analytics)
  • Computer Science Engineering (Spl- Software Engineering)

    • Duration: 2 Years

---------------------------------------------------------

Eligibility

B.E. or B.Tech. or equivalent Degree (e.g. AMIE, GRADE-IETE etc) in respective branches of Engineering and Technology with a First Class or equivalent CGPA.

DUAL DEGREE

  • Dual Degree B.Tech & M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

    • Duration: 5 Years

  • Dual Degree B.Tech & MBA in Computer Science & Engineering

    • Duration: 6 Years

---------------------------------------------------------

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed 10+2 examination in 2015, 2016 or 2017 with at least 60% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics taken together. Should have studied in regular full time formal education in their school / college. Should have been born on or after 01.07.1997.

POST GRADUATE

  • Post Graduate Doctoral ( Ph.D )

---------------------------------------------------------

Eligibility

Candidate having M.Tech / ME / MCA / MBA or equivalent Degree with minimum 60% marks or an equivalent CGPA or M.Sc./MA/M.Com/LLM or an equivalent degree with minimum of 55% marks.
